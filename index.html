The working bio.

J'myle has hosted and featured at the Tacoma Comedy Club and Emerald City Comedy Club in Seattle. He was selected for The Hollywood Comedy Store's Potluck Show — twice, in case you missed it the first time.

His comedy is autobiographical without being indulgent and clever without being cold. He writes the kind of material that earns the laugh on the second beat, then steals an extra one on the third. He has studied under Alice Fraser, Tiff Stevenson, and Andrew Frank.

He first stepped on a stand-up stage at Wiseguys Comedy Club's Trolley Square location in 2012. He has been refining the craft ever since.
